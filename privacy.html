1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ACFIX Delhi ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit acfixdelhi.co.in or use our services.

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using our website:

  • Name — To address you when we contact you.

  • Area / Locality — To arrange a technician visit in your area.

We may also automatically collect non-personal data including browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our site through Google Analytics and Google Ads tags.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

Your information may be shared with:

Our website uses cookies set by third-party services including Google Analytics and Google Ads. These cookies help us understand how visitors use our site and measure our advertising effectiveness. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

6. Data Retention

We retain your booking information only as long as necessary to complete your service request, typically no more than 90 days after your service date. You may request deletion of your data at any time.
