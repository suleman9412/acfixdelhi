FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the visiting charge for AC repair in Delhi NCR?▼
We charge ₹299 as a visiting and diagnosis fee. Parts and labour for the actual repair are quoted separately after inspection — and you decide whether to proceed.
Do you repair Split AC and Window AC both?▼
Yes. We repair both Split AC and Window AC for all major brands. Our technicians handle not-cooling issues, gas refill, water leakage, compressor problems, PCB repair, installation, and shifting.
Is same day AC repair available?▼
Yes. We offer same day AC repair across Delhi NCR. Call us to schedule — most visits happen within 2–4 hours of booking.
How much does AC gas refill cost in Delhi?▼
AC gas refill starts at ₹1,499 for R22 gas and ₹1,799 for R32 gas. This includes the gas and labour. Leak repair if needed is quoted separately after inspection.
Do you provide a warranty on repair work?▼
Yes. We provide a 30-day service warranty on all repair work. If the same problem recurs within 30 days, we will revisit and fix it at no additional service charge.
Are you an authorized or OEM service centre?▼
No. AC Fix Delhi is an independent third-party repair service. We are not affiliated with, authorized by, or endorsed by any AC manufacturer or their authorized service centres.
